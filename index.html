Frequently Asked Questions

Get answers to common questions about our roofing services

How long does a roof replacement take?

Most residential roof replacements take 1-3 days, depending on the size of your home, weather conditions, and complexity of the project. We'll provide a detailed timeline during your consultation.

Do you offer free roof inspections?

Yes! We provide comprehensive roof inspections at no cost. Our experienced professionals will assess your roof's condition and provide you with a detailed report and recommendations.

Are your services covered by warranty?

Absolutely! We stand behind our work with comprehensive warranties on both materials and workmanship. Warranty periods vary by service and materials used, which we'll discuss during your consultation.

What type of roofs do you work on?

We work on all types of roofing systems including asphalt shingles, metal roofing, tile, slate, and flat roofs. Our team has experience with both residential and commercial roofing projects.

Do you offer emergency repair services?

Yes, we provide 24/7 emergency roofing services for urgent situations like storm damage, leaks, or structural issues. Contact us immediately for emergency repairs.
