Seasonal Maintenance Checklist

Spring

  • Inspect for winter damage
  • Clean gutters and downspouts
  • Check for loose shingles
  • Inspect flashing and seals

Summer

  • Remove debris and moss
  • Check attic ventilation
  • Inspect for heat damage
  • Apply protective treatments

Fall

  • Clean gutters of leaves
  • Inspect for storm damage
  • Check chimney and flashing
  • Prepare for winter weather

Winter

  • Monitor for ice dams
  • Check for snow load issues
  • Inspect attic insulation
  • Emergency repairs as needed
